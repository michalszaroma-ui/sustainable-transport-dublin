Why it matters

Why This Survey Matters

Research and data collection are essential for improving public transport. The NTA and Dublin City Council regularly survey commuters to understand travel patterns and barriers to sustainable transport uptake. Your responses contribute to real understanding of what Dubliners need to make the switch from cars to greener alternatives.

📊

Evidence-Based Policy

Survey data directly informs transport investment decisions, helping planners prioritise new bus corridors, cycle lanes, and tram extensions.

🗣️

Your Voice Counts

Public consultation is a legal requirement for major transport projects in Ireland. Citizen input has already shaped the BusConnects and Luas expansion plans.

🌱

Drive Change

Every response helps build a picture of what it would take to get more Dubliners out of their cars and onto sustainable transport options.
